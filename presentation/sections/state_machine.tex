\begin{block}{Inductive invariant principle}
Prove $\varphi(s_0)$ and $\forall s, t \in \mathcal{T}.\ \varphi(s) \Rightarrow \varphi(t(s))$ --- no execution can reach a $\neg\varphi$ state.
\end{block}
